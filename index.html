Purpose

Our Mission

The Student Management Fund originated from tutoring international students across Ireland, the Middle East, and South Asia — and has since expanded into financial analysis, valuation, quantitative research, automation, and analytics. SMF operates as a network model, connecting clients globally with skilled professionals and students across Pakistan. Service providers retain 70% of all revenue generated; the remaining 30% is allocated to the fund. Our objective is to build Asia's leading student-managed fund, compounding returns through disciplined investment while channelling profits toward fund growth and female education access in Pakistan.
